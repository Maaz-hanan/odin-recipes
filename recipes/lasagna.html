Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles – 12 sheets
  • Ground beef – 500 g (1 lb)
  • Tomato sauce – 3 cups (about 700–800 g)
  • Ricotta cheese – 2 cups (450 g)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded) – 3 cups (350 g)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated) – ½ cup (50 g)
  • Onion – 1 medium (chopped)
  • Garlic – 2 cloves (minced)
  • Olive oil – 2 tbsp
  • Salt – 1 tsp
  • Black pepper – ½ tsp

Steps:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions, drain, and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef, cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with half of the Parmesan.
  6. In a baking dish:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
    • Add a layer of noodles.
    • Spread ricotta mixture.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella.
    • Repeat layers until dish is full.
    • Finish with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes until cheese is golden.
  9. Let rest 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
