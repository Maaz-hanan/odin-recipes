Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough – 1 (about 250 g for a medium pizza)
  • Olive oil – 1 tbsp
  • Tomato sauce – ½ cup (120 g)
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese – 200 g (sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves – 6–8
  • Salt – a pinch

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Roll out pizza dough and place on a baking tray.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with mozzarella slices.
  5. Bake for 8–12 minutes until cheese melts and crust is golden.
  6. Remove from oven, add fresh basil leaves, drizzle olive oil, and sprinkle salt.
