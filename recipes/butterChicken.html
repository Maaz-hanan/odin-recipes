Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken (boneless, cubed) – 500 g
  • Butter – 3 tbsp
  • Onion (finely chopped) – 1 large
  • Garlic (minced) – 3 cloves
  • Ginger (minced) – 1 tbsp
  • Tomato puree – 1 cup (240 g)
  • Fresh cream – ½ cup (120 ml)
  • Yogurt – ½ cup (120 g)
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder – 1 tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves – for garnish

Steps:

  • Marinate chicken with yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt. Set aside for at least 30 minutes.
  • Heat 2 tbsp butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until lightly browned. Remove and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add 1 tbsp butter, sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until golden.
  • Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  • Stir in garam masala and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  • Add the cooked chicken back into the pan and mix well.
  • Pour in fresh cream, stir, and simmer for 5–7 minutes on low heat.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
